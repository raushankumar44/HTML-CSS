GAYA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING, GAYA

Gaya College of Engineering (GCE), located in Gaya, Bihar, is a prominent public engineering institution with a rich history and a commitment to technical excellence.
Established in 1958 as Magadh Engineering College, it was re-established in 2008 under its current name and operates under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar. GCE is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University,Patna, and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) .

Campus and Infrastructure

Situated on an 87-acre urban campus in Sri Krishna Nagar, approximately 15 km from Gaya city center, GCE offers a serene and pollution-free environment conducive to learning. The campus includes two academic buildings, a workshop, a canteen, and a branch of Dakshin Bihar Gramin Bank to cater to the financial needs of students and staff .

Academic Programs

GCE offers undergraduate Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs in the following disciplines:

(1) Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

(2) Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

(3) Mechanical Engineering (ME)

(4) Civil Engineering (CE)

Admissions

Admissions to GCE's B.Tech programs are conducted through the Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (BCECE), a state-level entrance exam. Candidates must meet the eligibility criteria and secure qualifying marks to gain admission.

Placement and Career Opportunities

GCE has a dedicated Training and Placement Cell that works to provide students with placement and internship opportunities. In the 2023 placement drive, the highest salary package offered was ₹5 LPA, with an average package of ₹4.16 LPA. Top recruiters included HCL, TCS, Aviotron Aerospace, and Kube Infotech .

Student Life and Extracurricular Activities

Beyond academics, GCE encourages students to engage in various extracurricular activities. The college hosts seminars, workshops, and cultural events, fostering a holistic development environment. Student-led initiatives and clubs provide platforms for leadership and innovation.

Conclusion

Gaya College of Engineering stands as a beacon of technical education in Bihar, combining a rich legacy with modern educational practices. Its commitment to academic excellence, infrastructural facilities, and student development makes it a preferred choice for aspiring engineers in the region.
